What is Animal Crossing?





Animal Crossing: New Horizons is a relaxing and fun video game made by Nintendo for the
Nintendo Switch. In the game, you live on an island with friendly animal neighbors.
You can catch fish and bugs, decorate your home, plant trees, and explore the island.
The game follows real time, so it changes with the seasons and holidays. Since its release
in March 2020, it has sold over 47.8 million copies worldwide, making it the second best-selling
game on the Nintendo Switch. As of April 2025, it's still one of the most played Nintendo games,
ranking #4 based on monthly active users. Many people enjoy visiting friends' islands and sharing
their creations, making it a popular and creative game for players of all ages.
